What is Forestblock?

Forestblock is considered as one of the most challenging and skilled-based Minecraft Java server in history. We're departing from the Minecraft Vanilla experience by using a completely different storytelling style. Each player is assigned a class; whether you're a mage with strong magical potency and a vast attack range, or a warrior who prefers close-combat battles, we support a wide range of playing styles. All of these elements include the importation of a wide range of unique weapons, equipment, and items to aid in the coming battles.
